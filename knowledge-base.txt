This is an AI-powered platform that helps aspiring software engineers prepare for technical interviews.
We offer mock interviews that cover a wide range of topics, including algorithms, data structures, system design, and behavioral questions.
Our goal is to provide a realistic and effective way for candidates to practice and improve their interview skills.
Our services are available through our website and mobile app.
We also provide free resources, such as a blog with interview tips and a community forum where users can connect with each other.
