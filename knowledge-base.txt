HeadStarter AI is a company that helps aspiring software engineers prepare for technical interviews. We offer a platform with AI-powered mock interviews that cover a wide range of topics, including algorithms, data structures, system design, and behavioral questions. Our goal is to provide a realistic and effective way for candidates to practice and improve their interview skills.

Our services are available through our website and mobile app. We offer a variety of subscription plans to fit different needs and budgets. We also provide free resources, such as a blog with interview tips and a community forum where users can connect with each other.

If you have any questions or need help with our platform, please visit our troubleshooting page or contact our technical support team. We are always happy to help.
